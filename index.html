Computational and Methodological Work

Methods development focused on reproducibility, diagnostic testing, and conservative interpretation, with techniques transferable across scientific domains.

  • Design of falsifiable diagnostic tests for complex systems
  • Evaluation of uncertainty signals and failure modes in algorithmic outputs
  • Reproducible experimental pipelines and preregistered analysis protocols

Methodological Posture

My work emphasizes falsifiable diagnostics, conservative error characterization, and identification of failure domains in public astronomical data products. Projects are scoped to remain informative under null outcomes and to support downstream scientific use rather than broad interpretive claims.
